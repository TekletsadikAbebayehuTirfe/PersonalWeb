Hobbies

My Hobbies are not just hobbies; they are also my gateways that relieve me from pressure, sorrow or just a bad day. These are soccer, music and weightlifting. I started to develop my passion for soccer so early undet the influence of my brother Habtamu who is a number one Arsenal fan. Thanks to him I am also the fan of the gunners. was so little when Arsenal last won the premier league but I witnessed about 4 FA cups. My favourite player from the squad is the German magician Mesut Ozil. I always admired his vision which largely showed his intellegence on the pitch and I wish someday in some way I could express my respect towards him.

Music is my other favourite things to do. I listen almost all types of genres like rap, pop R&B, soul. oldies both from Ethiopia and abroad. I am a huge fan of renowned artists like Mohammud Ahmed,Teferi Negash, Aregahegn Worash, Neway Debebe and Teddy Afro. From females I enjoy listening to Gigi, Mikaya, and Zeritu. I am also a fan of music abroad; specially rap/ I am a huge fan of Eminem in this genre since he transformed this so called black music to a whole international level. So simply put music is my therapy.

Last but not least is weight lifting. I not only lift weights but also do cardio training for my endurance during football matches. I try to give my all on the pitch so inorder to do that I need to hit the gym at least 3 days a week to meet my standards as long as this schedule does not clash with my syllabus.
